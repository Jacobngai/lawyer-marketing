# Service: Conversion-Driven Lawyer Meta Advertising
**Tone**: Magnetic, Social-Proof, Retargeting-Heavy.

---

## Tier 1: The "Attention-Hijacker" Feed Strategy
- **Headline**: "Stop Being the 'Invisible Expert' on Social Media."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Attention as currency. Status.
- **Copy**: "Your potential clients are on Facebook and Instagram for 2 hours a day. If you aren't there with a compelling, high-authority message, your competitors are. We deploy magnetic visuals and headlines that stop the scroll and establish your presence instantly."
- **Image Idea**: A scrolling mobile feed that 'Pauses' on a strikingly authoritative legal ad.

## Tier 2: The "Retargeting Loop" (Second Chances)
- **Headline**: "Follow Your Prospects Everywhere Until They Are Ready to Hire."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Persistence. Omni-presence.
- **Copy**: "90% of your website visitors leave without calling. Our 'Retargeting Loop' follows them across the web with social proof, client testimonials, and authority pieces, ensuring your firm is the only name they remember when they are finally ready to take action."
- **Image Idea**: An infinity loop showing a lead moving from 'Initial Visit' to 'Re-engagement' to 'Contact'.

## Tier 3: The "Case-Specific" Funnel (Personalization)
- **Headline**: "Direct-Response Funnels Built for Your Specific Practice Areas."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Segmented marketing.
- **Copy**: "We don't do 'general' ads. We build dedicated funnels for Family Law, Criminal Defense, and Corporate Litigation. We ensure the right person sees the right message at the right time, maximizing your conversion rate and minimizing your 'cost per consultation.'"
- **Image Idea**: Three distinct funnel paths labeled with different legal specialties leading to one 'Gold Call' button.

## Tier 4: The "Social Authority" Multiplier
- **Headline**: "Turn Your Ads into a Self-Refining Engine of Market Authority."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Compound interest. Market authority.
- **Copy**: "Every ad we run builds your firm's social capital. We track engagement, comments, and shares to refine your messaging every 7 days. You don't just get leads—you get a brand that the market identifies as the premier choice."
- **Image Idea**: A 'Social Growth' chart showing engagement compounding over time.
