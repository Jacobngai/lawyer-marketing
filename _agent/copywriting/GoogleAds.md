# Service: ROI-Focused Google Ads for Lawyers
**Tone**: Aggressive, Precision, High-Intensity.

---

## Tier 1: The "Interceptor" Strategy (Search Intent)
- **Headline**: "Intercept Your Best Clients at the Exact Moment of Crisis."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Precision. Timing.
- **Copy**: "When someone searches for a 'divorce lawyer' or 'personal injury attorney' at 2 AM, they aren't 'browsing'—they are buying. We put your firm's solution directly in front of them the second they admit they need help, capturing the highest-intent leads before they even look at Page 2."
- **Image Idea**: A crosshair targeting the 'Search Bar' on a dark screen.

## Tier 2: The "Psychographic" Ad Copy (High-Trust Messaging)
- **Headline**: "Ads That Speak to the Heart (And the Hurtle) of the Matter."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Emotional resonance + direct response.
- **Copy**: "Most lawyer ads are boring, generic, and invisible. We craft direct-response copy that targets the specific psychological pain points of your prospects, forcing them to stop scrolling and realize: 'Finally, a lawyer who understands my situation.'"
- **Image Idea**: A side-by-side comparison of a 'Boring' ad vs our 'High-Impact' ad.

## Tier 3: The "Negative Keyword" Shield (Efficiency)
- **Headline**: "Stop Burning Cash on 'Cheap' and 'Information-Only' Searchers."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Cost control. Wealth focus.
- **Copy**: "We don't just find you leads; we filter out the noise. Our proprietary negative keyword lists ensure you never pay for a single click from 'free advice' seekers or 'low-value' cases. Every dollar you spend is focused on the highest-worth litigation."
- **Image Idea**: A shield reflecting 'Low Value' clicks away from a gold bar.

## Tier 4: The "Bid-Dominance" Algorithm (Scaling)
- **Headline**: "Own the Market share You Deserve with Intelligent, Dynamic Bidding."
- **Kennedy Logic**: Domination. Scaling.
- **Copy**: "We monitor the competitive landscape every hour of every day. We adjust your bids in real-time to ensure you dominate the prime search real estate during peak hours, then throttle back when the value drops. Strategic. Calculated. Scalable."
- **Image Idea**: A dynamic dashboard showing 'Market Share' climbing week-over-week.
