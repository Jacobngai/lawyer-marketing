# 📝 LawyerMarketing.my Content Brief Workflow

Every article generated for the blog must be validated against this brief to ensure it aligns with the "Midnight Luxe" brand and the "Trusted Advisor" strategy.

## 1. Audience Persona (Choose One)
*   [ ] **The Solo Practitioner (DIY/Tactical)**: Focus on low-cost, high-impact technical wins. Provide "How-to" steps.
*   [ ] **The Senior Partner (Strategic/ROI)**: Focus on authority, firm growth, technology (AI), and competitive advantage.

## 2. The Logical Scenario (The Socratic Hook)
*   Define the scenario that makes the channel choice "obvious."
*   *Example for SEO*: "In a police station at 2 AM, do you wait for a Facebook Ad or do you search Google?"
*   *Example for Branding*: "When a client is deciding between two firms with equal fees, which LinkedIn profile makes them feel 'safe'?"

## 3. The Technical Core (Choose Focus)
*   [ ] **SEO (Search)**: On-page, technical, backlink strategy.
*   [ ] **GEO (Local Maps)**: Proximity optimization, review management, local citations.
*   [ ] **AIEO (AI Search)**: Structuring data for ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini.
*   [ ] **Branding**: Photography, tone of voice, LinkedIn authority.

## 4. The "Small Win" (Actionable Tip)
*   Every post must include exactly one technical tip the reader can implement in under 10 minutes (e.g., "Change your GMB description to include [Location]").

## 5. Malaysian Regulatory Guardrail
*   [ ] Verify the content follows the *Legal Profession (Publicity) Rules*.
*   [ ] Ensure tone is "Professional & Dignified" (avoiding gimmicks/hard sales).

## 6. The Conversion Path
*   **CTA Type**: Cal.com Booking Link (Professional Consultation).
*   **Message**: Context-aware (e.g., "Discuss your High-Ticket Personal Brand").

---

**Logic Filter**: Before writing, ask: *"Does this make the lawyer look smarter for reading it, or does it sound like a generic marketing sales pitch?"* (Aim for the former).
