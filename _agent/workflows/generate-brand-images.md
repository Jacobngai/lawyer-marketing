# Generate Brand Images Skill

This workflow provides the standard guidelines, prompts, and constraints for generating on-brand graphics and infographics for `lawyermarketing.my` using the `generate_image` tool.

When the user asks you to generate a chart, graphic, or concept card, refer to these exact visual prompt templates and adhere strictly to the Do's and Don'ts.

## Core Brand Identity (Midnight Luxe)

### Color System
- **Deep Rich Gold:** `#D4AF37` (Primary accent)
- **Pitch Black:** `#0A0A0A` (Primary background)
- **Carbon Dark:** `#1A1A1A` (Cards & Panels)
- **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF` (Headlines / Callouts)
- **White 70% / 50%:** (Subheadings / Descriptions / Footnotes)

### Typography Rules
*Note: Include typography keywords in the prompt to encourage modern sans-serif fonts.*
- Keep it clean: "modern sans-serif", "bold headlines", "ALL CAPS for labels".

## Visual Prompts By Asset Type

When generating images, append or adapt the following base prompts to fit the specific data point you are visualizing. **Do not deviate from the style keywords.**

### 1. Data & Stats Line Chart
**Use this for:** Upward trends, volume growth, specific timelines.
**Base Prompt:**
> Dark dramatic infographic, pitch black background #0A0A0A, single glowing gold line chart trending upward, deep rich gold #D4AF37 data line with subtle glow, white axis labels, minimal grid, bold headline text in gold at top, y-axis labels, modern tech aesthetic, flat design, no gradients, professional legal marketing brand, clean minimal layout, no decorative elements

### 2. Comparison / CTR Bar Chart
**Use this for:** Comparing percentages, ranking positions, or 'Us vs. Them' metrics.
**Base Prompt:**
> Dark horizontal bar chart infographic, pitch black background, nested data with percentages, labels on left side in dark overlay, gold #D4AF37 primary highlight bar for top position, muted dark bars for lower positions, percentage values right-aligned in gold for top item, white for others, bold short title in white, modern tech-forward legal marketing brand, clean minimal layout, no gradients

### 3. Concept Explainer Card
**Use this for:** Explaining a standalone process, step, or feature text.
**Base Prompt:**
> Dark concept card infographic, pitch black background, left side: subtle dark photo of professional person at 30% opacity overlay, right side: vertical accent line, dark gold #D4AF37 text elements, white subheading letters, dark aesthetic, modern typography, tech forward layout, minimal layout, no decorative elements

### 4. Feature Grid Card
**Use this for:** 4-point grids, lists of benefits, or multi-step processes.
**Base Prompt:**
> Dark dramatic feature grid infographic, pitch black background, bold white headline at top, 2x2 grid of dark cards #1A1A1A with rich gold icon vectors left, white feature name text, thin gold #D4AF37 border on cards, modern tech forward aesthetic, flat clean rendering layout, clean geometric layout, subtle gold glow on card borders, professional, no gradients

## 🛑 Strict DO's & DON'Ts

### DO
- Use gold **only** for key data points, highlights, and accents.
- Keep backgrounds dark (`#0A0A0A` to `#1A1A1A` range).
- Use bold, modern sans-serif fonts.
- Add a subtle brand watermark/logo in the bottom right corner if possible.
- Use ALL CAPS for category labels and tags only.
- Keep details minimal — **one insight per visual.**

### DON'T
- Do not use bright green, neon, or bright colors other than gold.
- Do not fill huge blocks with gold color (use it as an accent).
- Do not use white or light backgrounds.
- Do not use more than 3 colors in one graphic.
- Do not use decorative, serif, or script typefaces.
- Do not clutter with too many data points or lines.
